Services Provided by Southern Sweet Treat Delights










Feel free to come join us in our relaxing dining area to spend time with friends and family. We offer free Wi-Fi if you would like to stop in and do a little bit of work while you enjoy some dessert. We have gluten, dairy, and sugar free options for satisfying all of your sweet tooth cravings. But at Souther Sweet Treat Delights we even offer a viewing area to watch the best local bakers prepare and decorate the best desserts around.


However, we are way more than just a bakery. We offer catering services for your next event. Wedding, birthday, or gathering, no party is too big or small for us to handle for you. Cookie platters, sample platters, specialty platters, wedding or birthday cakes are some catering services offered to you. Jump on over to the Contact Us page to reach out and schedule your event.



Subscription Services? I though you would never ask. Sign up for our monthly subscription services to receive tool kits, recipes, and guides to help elevate your culinary and patsry skills. Beginners to advanced skill sets, young in age to the young in heart, we have a service that is suitable for you. Mix and match what options you would like as we have something for every budget. Packages range from $5 per month to $50 a month. Go to the Contact Us page to set up your subscription today.



Date nights are our favorite nights. Come and enjoy a night of pure fun, bliss, and romance with our date night package. You and your special person get to choose a dessert you desire to make from a select menu which consists of several specialty items. You will have a personal pastry chef helping as much or little as you like. The package reserves a room specifically for you and your partner for conversation, laughs, and of course complimentary drinks. Only one couple per night and they book up fast. Date nights can range from one to three hours. Grab our next spot by going to the Contact Us page to schedule your evening soon.
